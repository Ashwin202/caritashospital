Caritas HDP Hospital: The Heartbeat of
Healthcare Excellence

 

In 1962, Caritas Hospital & Institute of Health Sciences opened its doors, ushering in a new era of accessible, high-quality healthcare for Central Kerala. This commitment to healthcare excellence has since expanded to include satellite hospitals like Caritas H.D.P. Hospital, Kaipuzha, which continues to deliver top-notch medical care to the community.

Formerly known as the House of Divine Providence Hospital, Caritas HDP Hospital boasts over 75 years of history. In April 2022, it became part of Caritas Hospital & Institute of Health Sciences. Located about 6 kilometers from Ettumanoor town, the hospital is equipped with advanced medical technology and a team of specialists, offering a range of services from surgery to cardiology.

Caritas H.D.P. Hospital is dedicated to providing quality healthcare at an affordable price, with services that encompass prevention, diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation, and palliative care. Our mission is to promote the health and happiness of our patients.
