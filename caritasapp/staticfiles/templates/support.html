Compassion in the Community: Caritas
Support at Your Service

The Caritas Support Team marks a groundbreaking initiative by Caritas Hospital, dedicated to offering compassionate support in various realms that demand a human touch. Comprised of self-motivated employees who voluntarily dedicate their time and effort, this team acts as a force for positive change.

Their mission is to directly engage and inspire individuals to participate in social initiatives and meaningful activities. As dedicated goodwill ambassadors of Caritas Hospital, they actively engage with communities and the public through various social interaction platforms. The Caritas Support Team embodies the spirit of compassion and brings a humanizing touch to our commitment to making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve.
