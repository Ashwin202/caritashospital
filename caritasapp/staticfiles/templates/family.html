Your Health, Our Legacy: Caritas Family Hospital,
Where Care Comes First.

 

Care is the bedrock of our institution, Caritas Hospital & Institute of Health Sciences, which was founded in 1962. The lack of accessibility to affordable healthcare led Mar Thomas Tharayil, the erstwhile Bishop of Kottayam, Kerala, to establish the Caritas Hospital & Institute of Health Sciences. This legacy is now further extended through at the Caritas Family Hospital in Kottayam.

We promise to provide next-gen medical care under a single roof. We continue to keep our promise by equipping ourselves with advanced medical technologies and a multitude of care facilities. A 24/7 emergency care unit with highly trained care experts backed by superior ambulance services is just a click away at Caritas Family Hospital. A fully integrated EMR expedites the diagnosis and prescription of medicines. Patient experience is as important to us as the clinical diagnosis. Keeping that in mind, the Caritas multispecialty family hospital features self-service kiosks for quick patient registration, convenient and contactless payments, home care services, video consultation, and online appointments. All these facilities heighten the overall patient experience.

Why Choose Caritas Family Hospital

Caritas Family Hospital in Kottayam promises to deliver the best healthcare services to all its patients in distress. Our 24/7 healthcare services are aimed to provide you with a holistic care experience that not only focuses on fixing your medical condition but also promises a faster recovery to help return to a healthy life. Here are a few reasons why you should choose Caritas Family Hospital, Kottayam.
