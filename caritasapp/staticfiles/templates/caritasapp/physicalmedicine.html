Where Healing and Innovation Unite in
Orthopaedic Excellence.

 

The Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation Department at Caritas offers a wide range of services to enhance and restore functional ability and quality of life in people with physical impairments/ disabilities of brain, spinal cord, nerves, bones, joints, ligaments, muscles, and tendons.

The expert PMR team led by physiatrists provides high quality, evidence-based care including physiotherapy and rehabilitative services with a multidisciplinary approach to patients of all ages.
