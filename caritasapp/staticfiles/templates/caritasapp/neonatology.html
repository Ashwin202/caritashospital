Balancing Hormones, Transforming Lives: Your Health, Our Expertise

 

The Neonatology Department is dedicated for the care of babies less than one month of age. The 15-bedded Neonatal Intensive Care Unit attached to the Delivery Suite is capable of dealing with all sorts of neonatal emergencies including surgical emergencies.

The Department offers high quality medical care to babies, based on various national and international guidelines.

The doctors at the Neonatology Department are well trained and qualified, with international experience.

Treatment and Procedures

Our Neonatology Department offers a wide array of specialized treatments and procedures to ensure the well-being of our youngest patients



  • Intubation & ventilation 
  • Surfactant administration 
  • Blood gas monitoring 
  • Total parenteral nutrition 
  • Phototherapy 
  • Exchange transfusion 
  • Neonatal surgery 
  • Neonatal hearing check 
  • Retinopathy of prematurity screening etc.
Facilities Offered

The Neonatology Department at Caritas Hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to ensure the well-being of our newborn patients.



  • Neonatal ventilators
  •  Radiant warmers 
  • LED phototherapy units 
  • Neonatal monitors 
  • Arterial blood gas analysers 
  • Neonatal echocardiogram 
  • Neonatal ultrasonogram
