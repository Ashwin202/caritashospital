Creating Confident Smiles,
One Procedure at a Time.

 

Caritas Hospital's Dental, Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery Department is a hub of expertise and dedication in the field of oral health. Our team comprises seasoned Maxillofacial surgeons, consultants, specialists, and skilled technicians who are committed to providing exceptional dental care

At the heart of our approach is a commitment to a multi-disciplinary methodology. This enables us to tailor our treatments to the unique needs of each patient, ensuring the highest quality of personalized dental care.

.Whether you require routine dental check-ups, oral surgery, or specialized treatments, we stand ready to deliver comprehensive solutions, always with a focus on your oral health and well-being.

Treatment and Procedures

The treatments offered by the Department of Dental, Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at Caritas Hospital are:

  • Implants (Single tooth implants, implant supported hybrid dentures and over dentures)

  • Periodontal surgeries

  • Dentures (full, partial, fixed, cast partial, immediate and over dentures)

  • Preventive treatments for dental caries in children (sealants and fluoride application)

  • Dental, cosmetic dental treatment (tooth colour filling, correction of spaced teeth in single visit)

  • Ceramic bridge and crowns

  • Root canal treatment (multiple and single visits)

  • Surgical removal of teeth

  • Maxillofacial surgeries
