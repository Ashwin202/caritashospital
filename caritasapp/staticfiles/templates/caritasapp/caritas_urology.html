Where Healing and Innovation Unite in
Orthopaedic Excellence.

 

Urology treatment in India is one of the most popular and demanded medical tourism packages. It is the branch of medicine concerned with the genitourinary system and deals with severe ailments like kidney disease, urinary tract infections, bladder dysfunction, erectile dysfunction and others. People from different countries come to India for affordable urology and urologic oncology treatment, which includes prostate cancer surgery and bladder cancer surgery. Caritas has achieved the name of the best urology hospital in Kerala, which is equipped with highly experienced specialists and state-of-the-art facilities. These facilities enable us at Caritas to carry forward the legacy of India being one of the most sought-after destinations for affordable urology treatments.

Over the years, we have reinstated our position as the best urology hospital in Kerala by being at the top of the latest innovations and technological refinements in the field of urological care. With our team of highly trained urologists, we provide state-of-the-art diagnostic services to all our patients. Our urologists are backed by many support staff dedicated to providing high-quality care, excellent service and personal attention to each patient.

Treatment and Procedures

Treatment and Procedures Caritas hospital offers urological care at all levels- from primary care to tertiary-level expertise. The urologists at the hospital use state-of-the-art technology to diagnose and treat their patients. Our urologists are highly experienced in the field of urology and provide a range of treatments, including:

  • Complete minimally invasive urinary stone management

  • Rigid and flexible ureteroscopy to treat ureteral obstruction or to place ureteral stents

  • Tr eatment of urinary tract stones of all sizes and locations

  • PCNL (Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy), MIP (Minimally invasive PCNL)

  • Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL)

  • Non-operative management for renal/ureteric stones

  • Lower Tract Endourology and Flexible Cystoscopy

  • Conduct urological procedures like urethroplasty, radical cystectomy, incontinence procedures, nephrectomy and partial nephrectomy

  • Laparoscopic and endoscopic procedures

  • Management of congenital problems like PUJ obstruction, obstructed megaureter etc.

  • Treatment for malignancies in kidney, ureter, bladder and prostate (Urologic Oncology)

  • Benign Enlargement of Prostate - TURP

  • Open Urology Procedures

  • Urethroplasty

  • Diagnosis and Treatment of Erectile Dysfunction

  • Treatment for Male Infertility

  • Andrology and Reconstructive

  • Renal Transplantation
