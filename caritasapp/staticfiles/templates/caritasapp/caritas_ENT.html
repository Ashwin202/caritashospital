Harmonizing Sound, Enhancing Lives:
Your Ear and Throat Health, Our Priority

 

The Department of Otorhinolaryngology (ENT) at the Caritas Hospital offers comprehensive care for all disorders related to the ear, nose, and throat. The Department is one of the best centers in Central Travancore providing:

  • General ENT surgeries including septoplasty, SMR, tonsillectomy, adenoidectomy, pre-auricular cyst excision, Caldwell-luc, dental cyst enucleation and cosmetic septorhinoplasty
  • Micro ear surgeries including tympanoplasty, mastoidectomy, modified radical mastoidectomy
  • Endoscopic nasal surgery (FESS) for chronic sinusitis, nasal polyp, DCR, benign tumors of nerve & nasopharynx
  • Micro-laryngeal surgeries for vocal cord polyp and vocal cord nodules, both benign and malignant
  • Management of all head & neck pathologies including benign and malignant tumors and abscess drainage
  • Therapeutic & diagnostic procedures for larynx & esophagus including foreign body removal
  • OPD consultation (available on all working days including Saturdays)
  • Nasal endoscopy & video-laryngoscopy done as outpatient procedures
  • Tests for hearing loss & giddiness( available on all working days)
  • Assessment, diagnosis and management of speech & language delay, done in Speech Therapy Department

Audiologists treat those with hearing loss and proactively prevent damage. Employing various testing strategies, audiologists aim to determine whether someone has normal sensitivity to sounds. If hearing loss is identified, audiologists determine which portions of hearing are affected, to what degree and where the lesion causing the hearing loss is found. In addition to diagnostic audiologic and vestibular pathologies , audiologists can also specialize in rehabilitation of tinnitus, hyperacusis, auditory processing disordes, cochlear implant use and hearing aid use.
