Where Healing and Innovation Unite in
Orthopaedic Excellence.

 

At Caritas Hospital, our Orthopaedics, Joint Replacement, and Arthroscopy Department offer cutting-edge treatments, including minimally invasive arthroscopy, interlocking nailing with advanced imaging, total joint replacements, Ilizarov ring fixators, and expert care for fractures and bone tumors. Our Level III emergency care unit, staffed by a dedicated team of emergency medicine, neurosurgery, orthopaedic, and general surgery specialists, provides 24/7 support.

 

We maintain a six-bed Emergency ICU with a 1:1 patient-to-nurse ratio and a specialized stainless steel laminar flow operation theater exclusively for orthopaedics, equipped with C-arm facilities for precision hip and spine surgeries. Caritas Hospital is committed to delivering top-notch orthopaedic and emergency care with skill and innovation.
