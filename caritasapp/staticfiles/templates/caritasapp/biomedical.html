Caritas Hospital: Safeguarding Health,
Safeguarding Earth

At Caritas Hospital, we prioritize the well-being of our patients, our staff, and the environment. Our dedicated Biomedical Waste Management program reflects our unwavering commitment to safe and responsible waste disposal.

We understand the critical importance of managing biomedical waste with utmost care. Our comprehensive approach ensures that all medical waste generated within our facility is handled, treated, and disposed of in a manner that safeguards public health and environmental integrity.

Caritas Hospital proudly operates in affiliation with IMAGE (Indian Medical Association Green Environment), further underlining our commitment to environmental responsibility. Our state-of-the-art systems and protocols guarantee compliance with all regulatory standards, ensuring the safety of our community and our planet. By adhering to the highest ethical and environmental standards, Caritas Hospital continues to set an example in responsible healthcare waste management.
