Comprehensive Respiratory Care for All Ages

The pulmonology department in Caritas Hospital is a specialized unit that completely treats respiratory diseases. The experienced doctors and nurses provide all diagnostic, therapeutic and rehabilitation services. We provide specialized care for children with respiratory conditions, adults with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), asthma and other respiratory diseases.

The primary purpose of the pulmonology department is to diagnose the patients and provide appropriate treatment to improve their quality of life. The aim is to prevent further complications, reduce hospitalization, improve physical activity and provide long-term control of symptoms.

Treatment and Procedures

Caritas Hospital provides highly effective treatment for recurring allergies, asthma, COPD, lung cancer, obstructive sleep apnoea and chronic ventilatory failure - especially those secondary to neuromuscular disorders. Our team of pulmonologists also provide specialized critical care for patients with acute respiratory conditions and respiratory failure:

  • Cryobiopsy for DPLDs
  • Foreign Body Removal
  • Pleural Biopsy
  • Forceps/Cryo Biopsy
  • Adhesiolysis
  • Mechanical/Chemical
  • Pleurodesis
  • Indwelling tunneled Pleural Catheter (IPC)
  • Pigtail Catheter
  • Conventional ICD
  • Haemoptysis management
  • BPF Management
  • Brachytherapy
  • EndoBrochial UltraSound (EBUS)
  • Mediastinal Node FNAC/FNAB
  • Staging EBUS for Lung Cancer
  • Peripheral Pulmonary Nodule (PPN) Evaluation
  • Thin Scope + Radial EBUS + Fluoroscopy
  • EUS-B-FNA
  • Pleural Biopsy
