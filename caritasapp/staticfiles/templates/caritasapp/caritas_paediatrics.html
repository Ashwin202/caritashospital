Pediatric and Neonatal Surgery

The Paediatric and Neonatal Surgery Division of Caritas Hospital deals with all surgical conditions in children below the age of 14 years.

Surgical treatments are offered for congenital malformations (birth defects) such as cleft lip, imperforate anus, tracheoesophageal fistula, diaphragmatic hernia, hirschsprung's disease, club foot etc.
