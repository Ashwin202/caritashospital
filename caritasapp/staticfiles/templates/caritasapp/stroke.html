Stroke Help Line, the 24-hour, on-call hyper acute stroke clinic of Caritas Hospital, is perhaps the only facility of its kind in the region. The expert doctors in the Clinic provide in-hospital evaluation, brain imaging (CT/MRI), and diagnosis and treatment initiation for stroke patients within 30 minutes of arrival. We also provide specialized care for Transient Ischemic Stroke (TIS). It is a condition where the patient shows stroke-like symptoms which subside by the time they are brought to hospital.

 

The Acute Stroke Clinic of Caritas Hospital has the following equipment and facilities:

 

  • CT Scanner
  • 1.5 Tesla MRI
  • Hybrid Cath Lab
  • Dedicated Neuro ICUs
  • Integrated Neurosurgery units
  • Comprehensive Stroke Centre
  • Dedicated Acute Stroke Team in Emergency
  • Cath-Lab with Neuro Interventional Procedures
  • Specialized Neuro Rehabilitation Unit
  • Video EEG & Sleep Lab
  • Aneurysm Coiling
  • Minimally Invasive Spine Surgeries
  • AWAKE Craniotomy
  • Stitchless Spine Surgery for Disc Prolapses
  • 19 Bedded Neuro Critical Care ICU
  • 24/7 Service from expert Neurologists Neuro Surgeons, Interventional Radiologist & Neuro Physiatrist
