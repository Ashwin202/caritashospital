Overview

 

Established in 2002, Caritas College of Nursing is a distinguished institution affiliated with the Kerala University of Health Sciences, Thrissur. Recognized and approved by the Kerala Nurses & Midwives Council and the Indian Nursing Council, the college is a bastion of excellence in nursing education. Offering a wide spectrum of programs, including B.Sc. Nursing, Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing, and MSc Nursing, Caritas College of Nursing is committed to shaping the future of healthcare professionals.

Managed by the esteemed Caritas Hospital Trust and under the auspices of the Catholic Archdiocese of Kottayam, this college came into being with the gracious patronage of H.E. Mar Kuriakose Kunnachery, Arch Bishop Emeritus of Kottayam.

In recognition of its significance, Caritas College of Nursing was declared a minority educational institution on December 11, 2007. The college operates under the umbrella of the Archdiocese of Kottayam, with Caritas Hospital as its parent institution. This 655-bedded hospital holds ISO 9001:2015 certification and is accredited by the National Accreditation Board for Hospitals (NABH), offering a wide range of specialty and super-specialty medical services.

The college has state of the art facilities, spacious classrooms with modern amenities and well equipped laboratories. An excellent line up of 30 qualified and experienced faculty with academic expertise offers training to the students. Students are provided a contusive learning environment that contributes to their overall development to a professional nurse.

Fostering a Legacy of
Compassionate Healthcare Professionals

The philosophy of the Caritas College of Nursing is consistent with the philosophy of Caritas Hospital Trust to which is affiliated. We believe that the education shall prepare the individual to provide preventive, promotive, curative and rehabilitative aspects of health care to the people of our country based on the basic scientific principles and thus enable to carry on the healing ministry of Jesus Christ and enable them to apply this knowledge in her personal and professional life.

We believe in preparing the students not only in professional skills, but also in their attitude and character upholding the dignity of the person with humanness and compassion for patients and their family. The student is an adult responsible for her own learning and has a right to be treated with dignity and respect. Therefore, a stimulating and challenging environment with ample opportunities for learning is provided in real life situations.
