Overview

 

Caritas College of Pharmacy was established in 23rd March 1981 under the eminent guidance and vision of His excellency Mar Kuriakose Kunnassery , the then Bishop of Kottayam. Now the new building located at 101 Junction, Kottayam, nearly 1.5 kms from the Caritas Hospital, is the new educational institution, where classes are slated to start the degree program as well as diploma program too, to provide quality education, which is the need of the hour.

This college offers D. Pharm and B. Pharm courses, recognized and approved by Pharmacy council of India, All India Council for Technical Education and affiliated to Directorate of Medical Education and Kerala University of Health Sciences, Thrissur.

This institution was started with an intention to create professionally qualified, technically competent, spiritually matured, psychologically integrated and socially acceptable pharmacists with professional ethics, good human values and national outlook.

At Caritas College of Pharmacy, our vision is deeply rooted in the pursuit of value-based education in the field of Pharmacy, driven by inspiration and a relentless commitment to academic and research excellence. We are dedicated to shaping the next generation of pharmacy professionals who will play a pivotal role in fostering a healthier society.

Shaping Pharmacy Professionals
with a Vision for Excellence and a Heart for Service.

Our mission encompasses several core objectives: first, to provide a comprehensive education that equips our students with the knowledge and skills necessary to contribute to the well-being of our communities. Second, we prioritize the preparation of both our staff and students to meet the ever-evolving challenges of the pharmacy profession. Lastly, we actively foster collaborations among clinical practice, industry, and our institution, believing that such partnerships yield mutual benefits and enrich the learning experience.

At Caritas College of Pharmacy, we are driven by these principles, ensuring that our graduates are not only well-qualified but also poised to make a positive impact on the world of healthcare and pharmaceuticals.
