How it Works

Fill up the online form or call us to book your appointment.

  • Begin the process by filling out the online form with your details or reach us at +91 99468 56789
  • This step is your initial point of contact to schedule a video consultation.

Choose a convenient schedule for appointments.

  • After you submit the form, our team will promptly contact you to provide available time slots and options.
  • Select a date and time that suits your availability from the available options

Make payment online, using the link shared through Email/SMS.

  • After selecting your appointment, you will receive a secure payment link through Email and SMS.
  • Click on the link to proceed with the online payment process.

After successful payment, appointments will be confirmed.

  • Once your payment is successfully processed, your appointment will be confirmed.
  • You will receive a confirmation notification through Email and SMS.

Make video consultation using the link shared, on a given time schedule.

  • On the scheduled date and time, click the link provided for your video consultation.
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection and a compatible device for the video call.
