Caritas KMM Hospital: Transforming Lives
through Exceptional Medical Care.

 

Caritas K.M.M. Hospital originated from a charitable general clinic that was started in 1985 and owned by Puthenangady Kurisupally of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church. An eye clinic was also later added as a service under this charitable general clinic. Soon, the authority shifted the clinic to a new premise in 1995. In 2017 Kurisupally Medical Mission was formed to manage the clinic. Caritas Hospital & Institute of Health Sciences took over the management of the charitable clinic in April 2021 that continued operating under the banner of Caritas K.M.M. Hospital.

At Caritas K.M.M. Hospital, we provide a holistic environment of care by offering excellent medical attention with our expert doctors and nurses. Caritas K.M.M. Hospital is located at Puthenangady near Kurishupally Kavala. The hospital is situated just 1.4 kilometers ( 4 minutes) away from Old Muncipal Bus Stand, Thirunakkara. And it is only 3.5 kilometers from Kottayam Railway Station . The strategic location of this hospital makes high-quality and affordable medical care accessible to people across Central Kerala.

Why Choose Caritas K.M.M. Hospital

aritas KMM Hospital has an experienced team of doctors dedicated to providing high-quality healthcare services that are affordable for everyone. The advanced technology used at the hospital helps doctors treat patients with utmost precision and accuracy. When you visit Caritas KMM Hospital, you can expect a prompt diagnosis of your medical condition and effective treatment options to help you get back to normal as soon as possible!
