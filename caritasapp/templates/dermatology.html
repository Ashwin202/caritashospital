Whether you're dealing with common skin ailments or more complex dermatological issues, you can trust that the Department of Dermatology at Caritas Hospital is equipped to provide

Whether you require medical treatment for dermatological conditions or seek cosmetic dermatology services, Caritas Hospital's Department of Dermatology is here to deliver excellence in care, utilizing the latest advancements in the field to promote healthy, radiant skin.

Treatment and Procedures

The treatments and services offered by the Department include:

  • Microdermabrasion

  • Chemical peeling

  • Facial rejuvenation

  • Punch grafting

  • Biopsy

  • Cryosurgery

  • Radiosurgery

  • Electro-surgery

  • Painless injection for keloids, Iontophoresis, and effective treatment for pigmentation, vitiligo, hair loss/ hair fall, unwanted hair, moles, warts, skin tags, corns and calluses are also done here.
